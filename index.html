Who We Are!

#Our coding for Us# to make Sri Lanka a better place! We are a nonprofit organisation with the vision of gathering motivated first-timers and veterans in technology field and working towards one common goal, making a better Sri Lanka. By defenition, from the start of a project to it's end, you can contribute to inspire change. We are on mission to see Sri Lanka as everyone would like to see it.
Work for the people, by the people, in this digital age.
